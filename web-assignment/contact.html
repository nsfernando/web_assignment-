Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

What information should I include in my message?

Please provide as much detail as possible about your inquiry, including any error messages, account information, or specific questions you have.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes! Our phone support is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. Emergency support is available 24/7.

Can I schedule a call or meeting?

Absolutely! For complex issues or business inquiries, we're happy to schedule a call. Just mention this in your message.

Is there a knowledge base or documentation?

Yes, we have extensive documentation and tutorials available in our help center. Check there first for quick answers.

How do I report a security issue?

For security-related concerns, please use the "Bug Report" subject or contact us directly at security@secureapp.com.
